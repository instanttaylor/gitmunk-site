What it does

Stop checking repos one by one.

Gitmunk watches the folders where you keep your clones and gives you one place to see — and fix — what's fallen behind.

04 — On-device digest

“What changed this week?”Very beta

Ask in plain language and get a real summary of the week's commits — features, fixes, the diffs that matter. Runs on Apple's on-device model, so it requires a Mac with Apple Intelligence.

Digest
What changed in acorns this week?
Three themes: a new caching layer for the acorn index ([a1c4f]), a fix for the duplicate-stash bug on cold start, and groundwork for multi-tree support. Nothing risky — all squirreled away cleanly. Runs on your Mac — code never leaves the device

Your code stays on your Mac.

The summaries run entirely on-device with Apple Intelligence. No servers, no uploads, no telemetry — nothing about your repositories ever leaves your machine.
